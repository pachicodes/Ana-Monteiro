About Ana Monteiro

Ana Monteiro is a knowledged, charismatic and self-motivated Newborn Care specialist.

Her calm and positive demeanor combined with her gentle guidance offer a great support for families who are creating their routines and adapting to this new phase.

She is an avid learner who is always participating in discussion forums, conferences and taking classes to expand her knowledge and keep herself updated, her latest qualification is as a Nurse Aide.

During her free time, you may find her walking her dog or riding a bike at the park, or if the weather is not the best, a good movie will keep her entertained.

Ana speaks English, Portuguese and Spanish.
