Services

Ana Monteiro is passionate about helping new parents and their babies to get adjusted in their new family setting, so she offers a variety of services based on your particular needs.

Newborn Care

Ana will care for your newborn overnight while you get the rest you need.

Service inludes but is not limited to:

  • Support of breastfeeding or bottle feeding,
  • Burping and changing,
  • Attending to monitors,
  • Preparing and cleaning bottles,
  • Keeping a log of baby's patterns, including eating, sleeping, elimination, and mood,
  • Sleep Conditioning.

PostPartum Doula:

Ana will give you the help you need during your recovery time and first weeks as a new parent as you get back into your regular routine.

Service inludes but is not limited to:

  • Preparing and cleaning bottles,
  • Burping and changing,
  • Development activities,
  • Laundry,
  • Meal Prep.

Newborn Consultation

Have questions or need advice? Set up a chat with Ana and get the answers and advice you need.
