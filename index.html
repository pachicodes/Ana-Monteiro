Welcome!

Are you a new parent or parent to be looking for support on this new phase and the best care for your baby?

Meet Ana Karina:

About Ana:

Ana has the typical Brazilian charisma, she is warm and self-motivated.

Her calm and positive demeanor combined with her gentle guidance will help you through the overwhelming "fourth trimester" and offer a great support to create your routines and adapt to this new phase.

She will bring evidence-based suggestions to help you make your informed decisions and become more confident taking care of your little one.

Services

Ana Monteiro is passionate about helping new parents and their babies to get adjusted in their new family setting,
having in mind how unique each family is, she offers a variety of services based on your particular needs.

Newborn Care

Ana will care for your newborn overnight while you get the rest you need.
She can help you with:

  • Support of breastfeeding or bottle feeding,
  • Burping and changing,
  • Attending to monitors,
  • Preparing and cleaning bottles,
  • Keeping a log of baby's patterns, including eating, sleeping, elimination, and mood,
  • Sleep Conditioning.

PostPartum Doula:

Get the help you need during your recovery time and first weeks as a new parent.
Service inludes but is not limited to:

  • Preparing and cleaning bottles,
  • Burping and changing,
  • Development activities,
  • Laundry,
  • Meal Prep.
